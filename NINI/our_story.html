How We Met

It all began on a sunny afternoon at my mada's house. We were both staring at the abyss and from the moment we locked eyes, we knew,the stove was left on .

Over the next few months, we grew chest hair, sharing countless heroin needles, stale beers, and dreams. Every moment felt like a new entry in our parol officer's report.

On march 42, zarathustra proposed to nini behind the post office in embakasi. It was a day filled with love, epinephrine, and the promise of DMT.

Today, we continue to build our life together, cherishing every mosquito bite and looking forward to the weekend beers.
